E S A AWAKENS ROSSETA - PHILAE


On 16th January 2014 at 10.00 a.m. The European Space Agency brought

Satellite Rosetta - Philae out of hibernation mode, to make ready for its

rendezvous with Comet 67P Churyumov-Gerasimerko in December 2014.


Launched in March 2004 onboard an Arianne 5 Rocket the Rosetta Satellite

has had an eventful journey to date when, on an Earth Flyby in 2007 it was

mistaken to be an Earth bound Asteriod !


The Probe “PHILAE” is scheduled to land on Comet 67P on December 20th 2014


What a wonderful Christmas present !!
