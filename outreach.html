PUBLIC OUTREACH

 

One of the Society’s main aims is to pass on its members’ understanding, excitement and enthusiasm for astronomy to an increasing number of people.

 

We achieve this through an ongoing programme of public events, visits to schools, interest groups and other outreach initiatives.

 

If you are interested, please do not hesitate to contact us.

CHOOSING A TELESCOPE

 

Newcomers to the Society do not have to own a telescope or even a pair of binoculars- though binoculars are ideal for getting started with astronomical observations. Certainly, before incurring any expense, it is advisable to discuss your needs with other members of the Society.

Elsewhere on this site (Resources) there are lists of books and equipment available on loan from the Society.
