Welcome


The aim of the Kings Lynn and district Astronomy Society is to promote all aspects of astronomy, for all ages, beginners and experienced, and to

provide a suitable location where all interested can meet and share knowledge and experience.


The Society hold meetings at Tottenhill Village Hall twice monthly which feature talks by both guest and society speakers. There are also regular

observation nights, weather permitting, for members and non members.


Please feel free to look around our site. Any comments or suggestions you might have will be most welcome. You will find information for our

meeting dates, upcoming events, and astrophotography taken by our members.


If you would like to come to a meeting or observation evening, or join our society, please Contact Us


Thank you for visiting.

News

STAR GAZING LIVE - A HUMONGOUS SUCCESS !!!


Late into the afternoon with clear Skies and a setting Sun, The King’s Lynn & District Society opened its

doors to the public for 2014’s SGL Event, “ A Journey into Space. ”


Opened by their Worships the Mayors of the Borough Council of King’s Lynn and West Norfolk and

Downham Market Town Council and MP’s Mr Henry Bellingham and Ms Elizabeth Truss, over 300 parents and

children came along to view the night sky.


With exhibitions going on inside the Hall, with Tea, Coffee and with help from “ The Fryer - Fish and Chip Van, ”

visitors were soon warmed up from the chill of the evening.

Kings Lynn and district Astronomy Society supports the British Astronomical Association Campaign for Dark Skies

and is a member of the Federation of Astronomy Societies
